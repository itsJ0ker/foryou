## üí° Tips While Writing

### For the Story:
- Use real dates if you remember them
- Include how YOU felt, not just what happened
- Be specific about moments
- Show progression from meeting to now

### For What I Love:
- Mix big things and small things
- Include quirks and imperfections
- Be specific, not generic
- Show you really pay attention

### For the Letter:
- Write like you talk
- Don't try to sound poetic if that's not you
- Share a fear or vulnerability
- Be honest about your feelings
- Don't make promises you can't keep
